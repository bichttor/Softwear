Returns

Q: What is Softwear's return policy?

A: To return eligible items, you must request a refund within 14 days of the delivery date, and return the items within 21 days from the delivery. For refunds, a $6.95 shipping fee will be deducted from the money you recieve, and returns for store credit will not have any shipping fees. Once we recieve your item, balance will be added to your account within 7 days.
